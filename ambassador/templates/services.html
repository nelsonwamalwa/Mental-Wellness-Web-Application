Our Services

Primary Mental Health Disorders We Treat

We provide comprehensive treatment for a variety of primary mental health disorders including Anxiety, Depression, PTSD, Bipolar Disorder, OCD, and Schizophrenia.

Substance Abuse Programs

Our programs include detox treatments for alcohol, cocaine, heroin, meth, and prescription drugs. We also offer residential inpatient programs and various therapy options including CBT and DBT.

Addiction Treatment Options

Our industry-leading intensive outpatient and residential treatment facilities in Orange County offer evidence-based alcohol and drug treatment options for those hoping to achieve long-term sobriety.

We provide professional evaluation & medical screening, drug & alcohol detox programs, 24/7 supervision, behavioral therapy, and group counseling. We also offer life skills development to help patients reintegrate into society and prevent relapse.
